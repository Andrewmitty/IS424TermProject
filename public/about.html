About Sugar and Lime


Handcrafted Desserts Made with Love




Our Story


Sugar and Lime is a family-owned restaurant bakery that has been serving delicious desserts and food. We take pride in our handcrafted treats made with love and only the finest ingredients. Our story began with a vision to create a welcoming space where friends and family can gather to savor unforgettable culinary experiences. Our skilled chef and baker Farheen works tirelessly to create unique and innovative recipes that are both visually stunning and incredibly delicious. From decadent cakes and pastries to savory sandwiches and salads, we strive to offer a diverse menu that caters to a variety of tastes and preferences.


Our Values and Mission


At Sugar and Lime, we are passionate about crafting delectable desserts and scrumptious savory dishes that delight the senses. Our restaurant/bakery is a haven for food lovers who appreciate the perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors, created with the finest ingredients and expert culinary skills. At Sugar and Lime, we take pride in using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients sourced from local farmers and suppliers. Our commitment to sustainability and ethical practices is reflected in every dish we create. We believe that food is not just about nourishment, but also about creating memorable moments and building connections with our customers. Beyond our culinary creations, our warm and inviting ambiance provides the perfect setting for gatherings, celebrations, and special occasions. Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite, indulging in a leisurely meal, or picking up treats for your loved ones, our friendly and attentive staff are dedicated to providing exceptional service that leaves a lasting impression.


Our Invitation To You


Join us at Sugar and Lime, where we blend our passion for food with our commitment to hospitality, to create an unforgettable dining experience that will tantalize your taste buds and capture your heart. We look forward to welcoming you to our restaurant/bakery and sharing our love for food with you.
